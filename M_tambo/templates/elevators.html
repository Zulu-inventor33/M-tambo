Elevators Maintenance with M-TAMBO

Elevators are critical components in modern high-rise buildings, providing essential vertical transportation. Proper maintenance of elevators ensures their safety, reliability, and efficiency. M-TAMBO, our comprehensive Maintenance Tracking and Documentation Software, offers a range of benefits for all parties involved in elevator maintenance.

1. Benefits for Developers/Building Owners

Real-Time Tracking: M-TAMBO allows building owners to monitor the status and maintenance schedules of all elevators across their properties. This helps in identifying potential issues before they become serious problems.

Maintenance History: Access detailed maintenance logs and history for each elevator, enabling building owners to track performance and service history over time.

Maintenance Scheduling: Easily schedule and track upcoming maintenance tasks to ensure compliance with safety regulations and prevent unexpected breakdowns.

Download Logs: Download electronic maintenance logs directly from the system for record-keeping, auditing, and insurance purposes.

Monitoring Maintenance Companies: View and manage the maintenance companies responsible for servicing elevators, ensuring they meet performance standards and contractual obligations.

2. Benefits for Maintenance Companies

Integrated Calendar: M-TAMBO provides an integrated calendar to manage and schedule all elevator maintenance jobs, ensuring efficient allocation of resources and timely service.

Technician Tracking: Track the assignments and performance of technicians, ensuring that all tasks are completed as scheduled and with the required quality.

Job Management: Manage and update job statuses, view details of each job, and communicate with building owners through the platform.

Electronic Logs: Generate and download electronic maintenance logs to streamline invoicing and keep accurate records of all maintenance activities.

Contract Management: View a list of all buildings where the company is contracted to perform maintenance, helping in planning and resource management.

3. Benefits for Technicians

Maintenance Calendar: Access a personalized calendar to view and manage assigned elevator maintenance tasks, ensuring they stay organized and on schedule.

Easy Data Entry: Enter maintenance activities and log details into the system quickly, reducing paperwork and administrative overhead.

Job Status Updates: Update the status of maintenance jobs directly in the system, keeping all stakeholders informed of progress and completion.

Generate Logs: Create and generate electronic maintenance logs with just a few clicks, making it easier to document and report on completed work.

System Features for Elevator Maintenance

  • Dashboard: Custom dashboards for building owners, maintenance companies, and technicians to access relevant information and perform tasks efficiently.
  • Alerts and Notifications: Automated alerts and notifications for upcoming maintenance tasks, overdue jobs, and other critical events.
  • Data Analytics: Analyze maintenance data to identify trends, predict potential issues, and improve overall elevator performance.
