Revolutionize Your Maintenance Management using the M-TAMBO CMMS

M-TAMBO is a computerized maintenance management tool that makes it simple to oversee and maintain your HVAC systems, elevators, and backup power generators from a single, integrated platform. Designed for ease of use, it streamlines your operations by allowing you to track, schedule and document maintenance issues efficiently, all in one place.

Easily manage and maintain HVAC systems, elevators, and power backup generators from one unified platform.

Centralized Dashboard to offer Real-Time Facility Maintenance Management and Tracking

Our dashboard provides a comprehensive, user-friendly interface where all users can easily access and manage their facility’s HVAC, elevator, and power systems. It offers real-time monitoring of system performance, intuitive scheduling for maintenance tasks, and quick access to critical alerts and reports

Learn More

Maintenance Calendar: Effortless Scheduling and Tracking at a Glance

Stay on top of your maintenance tasks with our intuitive calendar feature. It allows you to easily schedule, track, and manage all maintenance activities for your HVAC systems, elevators, and power backup generators. With clear visual timelines and reminders, you'll never miss a maintenance deadline, ensuring your systems remain in peak condition and operational efficiency is maintained.

Cloud-Based Storage: Secure, Accessible Records Anytime, Anywhere

Our system uses cloud technology to ensure all your maintenance records, system data, and historical information are safely stored and easily retrievable from anywhere. Enjoy seamless access, automatic backups, and enhanced data security, providing you with peace of mind and reliable access to critical information whenever you need it.

Seemless integration with external accounting softwares to enhance financial management

Our platform connects effortlessly with popular accounting tools, enabling automatic synchronization of maintenance expenses, budget tracking, and financial reporting. This integration simplifies the accounting process, reduces manual data entry, and provides a comprehensive view of your facility’s financial health, ensuring accurate and efficient financial management.
